About Me

Hi, my name's Theo, and I have a passion for development and design. It started from when I was little. I saw and played numerous creative games that inspired me with many concepts of my own. I yearned to be able to one day bring my own visions to life. Then, at the beginning of high school I began to teach myself python and eventually took on GCSE Computer Science. At this point I had realised that it really was possible for me to make my dreams come true. Currently, I'm continuing to pursue this dream as I progress through a College T-Level in Digital Software Development. During 2023, I found myself interested in both 3D and 2D design and began to teach myself how to use Blender and Figma. I haven't produced much finished results, however, I have certainly gained a lot of experience over the time.
I hope I haven't bored you already.
